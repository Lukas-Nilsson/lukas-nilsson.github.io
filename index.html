About Me

I'm a product designer and engineer based in Melbourne, blending creativity with technical expertise to build digital products that delight users. My approach combines user-centered design methodologies with data-driven decision making.

With experience at ANZ and The Human Archives, I've led projects spanning fintech platforms, cultural preservation initiatives, and interactive experiences that connect people with stories. Each project applies systematic design thinking and rigorous testing protocols to ensure measurable outcomes.

I focus on creating meaningful digital experiences that solve real problems, employing user research, prototyping, and iterative design processes to build products that make a genuine impact.
