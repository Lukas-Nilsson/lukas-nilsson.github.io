Data Visualization Side Project

Near-Earth Objects Visualization

Interactive 3D visualization of potentially hazardous asteroids and meteorites based on NASA's CNEOS Sentry data. Features realistic orbital mechanics with accurate relative sizes and distances for the Sun, Earth, Moon, and meteorites.

Loading 3D visualization...

Controls

Mouse: Click and drag to rotate the view around Earth

Scroll: Zoom in and out (2-50 units)

Click: Click on meteorites to see detailed information

Trails: Colored trails show past orbital paths

Future Paths: Dotted lines show predicted future trajectories

Tails: White particle tails show movement direction and speed

Data Source

This visualization uses data from NASA's CNEOS Sentry system, which tracks potentially hazardous near-Earth objects.

Color Coding

High Threat (Torino Scale > 0)
Moderate (Palermo Scale > -2)
Low Threat
